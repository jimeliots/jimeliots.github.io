ABOUT


JC RENEWABLES (OPC) Pvt. Ltd was established to address the growing demand for design and consulting services in the BIPV sector. The firm focuses on specialist design of integrated BIPV systems for futuristic buildings that complements the architect’s design intend. It  also take up electrical design projects and related engineering works and serves as a business coordinator for roof top and ground mount commercial, residential and utility scale solar PV projects across the globle.
